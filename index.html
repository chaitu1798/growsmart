Plant of the Month: Sunflower

Sunflowers love full sun and well-drained soil. Water regularly for tall, happy blooms!

Introduction to Horticulture

Horticulture is the art and science of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, and ornamental plants. It's a rewarding hobby and a vital skill for anyone interested in healthy living, sustainability, and connecting with nature. For beginners, horticulture offers a hands-on way to learn about plant care, food production, and the environment.

Mission of the Project

GrowSmart is here to empower you—whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned gardener—to grow smarter, greener, and happier gardens. Our friendly platform brings together practical tips, inspiring stories, and a supportive community to help you cultivate your skills and nurture a lifelong love for plants. Let's grow together!

Tips of the Month

June Tip: As summer heats up, mulch your garden beds to retain moisture and keep roots cool. Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation and help plants thrive during hot days!
